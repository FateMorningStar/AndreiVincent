About the Luxmundi Cup 2024

"What is Luxmundi Cup?"

Luxmundis Cup is an Event were the different institute's of the TCGC Global College compete with each other from different events like games,dances,short Movies,Mr.and Ms Luxmundis, musical bands and etc. to prove which institute would come out on top and dominate the other institute's

"Was it a good experience?"

Short Answer "YES" especially the opening of the event was memorable for everyone even though my institute didn't win much from the event's it was still fun and i can't wait for next year.
